## Plastic Materials

### PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.33-1.41 g/cm³
- Melting point: 250-260°C
- Glass transition: 70-80°C
- Clarity: Excellent (transparent)
- Barrier: Good oxygen barrier, moderate moisture barrier

**Applications**:
- Beverage bottles (water, soft drinks)
- Food containers
- Pharmaceutical packaging
- Cosmetic bottles

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1630
- EU 10/2011 compliant
- BPA-free

**Recyclability**: ♳ (Code 1) - Highly recyclable

---

### HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene)

**Properties**:
- Density: 0.94-0.97 g/cm³
- Melting point: 120-130°C
- Clarity: Opaque to translucent
- Barrier: Excellent moisture barrier, poor oxygen barrier
- Chemical resistance: Excellent

**Applications**:
- Milk jugs
- Detergent bottles
- Cosmetic containers
- Industrial containers

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1520
- EU 10/2011 compliant
- Food contact approved

**Recyclability**: ♴ (Code 2) - Highly recyclable

---

### LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene)

**Properties**:
- Density: 0.91-0.94 g/cm³
- Melting point: 105-115°C
- Flexibility: High
- Clarity: Translucent
- Barrier: Good moisture barrier

**Applications**:
- Squeeze bottles
- Flexible packaging
- Films and bags
- Cosmetic tubes

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1520
- EU 10/2011 compliant

**Recyclability**: ♶ (Code 4) - Recyclable

---

### PP (Polypropylene)

**Properties**:
- Density: 0.89-0.92 g/cm³
- Melting point: 160-165°C
- Chemical resistance: Excellent
- Heat resistance: Good (up to 100°C)
- Clarity: Transparent to opaque

**Applications**:
- Bottle caps and closures
- Food containers (hot fill)
- Pharmaceutical packaging
- Cosmetic jars

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1520
- EU 10/2011 compliant
- Autoclavable grades available

**Recyclability**: ♵ (Code 5) - Recyclable

---

### PS (Polystyrene)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.04-1.06 g/cm³
- Clarity: Crystal clear
- Rigidity: High
- Barrier: Poor
- Cost: Low

**Applications**:
- Food containers (non-hot fill)
- Cosmetic compact cases
- Display packaging

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1640
- EU 10/2011 compliant

**Recyclability**: ♸ (Code 6) - Limited recyclability

---

### PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.3-1.45 g/cm³
- Flexibility: Rigid to flexible (with plasticizers)
- Chemical resistance: Good
- Barrier: Good

**Applications**:
- Blister packaging
- Clamshells
- Shrink film

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1975
- EU 10/2011 compliant
- Plasticizer restrictions apply

**Recyclability**: ♹ (Code 3) - Difficult to recycle

**Note**: Environmental concerns due to chlorine content

---

### PETG (PET Glycol-modified)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.27 g/cm³
- Clarity: Excellent (crystal clear)
- Impact resistance: Superior to PET
- Chemical resistance: Good
- Processability: Easier than PET

**Applications**:
- Cosmetic jars
- High-clarity bottles
- Display packaging
- Medical packaging

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 177.1630
- EU 10/2011 compliant

**Recyclability**: ♷ (Code 7) - Recyclable with PET

---

### PC (Polycarbonate)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.20-1.22 g/cm³
- Impact resistance: Excellent
- Clarity: Excellent
- Heat resistance: Up to 135°C
- UV resistance: Poor (requires coating)

**Applications**:
- Baby bottles (BPA-free grades)
- Water cooler bottles (5-gallon)
- Medical devices

**Regulatory**:
- FDA approved (BPA-free grades)
- EU 10/2011 compliant

**Recyclability**: ♷ (Code 7) - Limited recyclability

**Note**: BPA concerns in legacy formulations

---

### PLA (Polylactic Acid)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.24 g/cm³
- Source: Renewable (corn starch, sugarcane)
- Biodegradability: Compostable under industrial conditions
- Heat resistance: Low (max 50-60°C)
- Clarity: Good

**Applications**:
- Food service containers
- Short shelf-life products
- Eco-friendly packaging

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 178.3297
- EU 10/2011 compliant

**Recyclability**: Compostable (industrial)

---

### ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene)

**Properties**:
- Density: 1.04-1.07 g/cm³
- Impact resistance: Excellent
- Rigidity: High
- Surface finish: Excellent
- Heat resistance: Moderate

**Applications**:
- Cosmetic compacts
- Luxury packaging
- Durable containers

**Regulatory**:
- FDA 21 CFR 181.32
- Not for direct food contact

**Recyclability**: ♷ (Code 7) - Limited recyclability

---

## Barrier Films and Specialty Materials

### EVOH (Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol)

**Properties**:
- Oxygen barrier: Excellent
- Moisture sensitivity: High
- Typically used in multilayer structures

**Applications**:
- Food packaging (meat, cheese)
- Pharmaceutical packaging
- Oxygen-sensitive products

---

### PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride)

**Properties**:
- Oxygen barrier: Excellent
- Moisture barrier: Excellent
- Typically used as coating

**Applications**:
- Food wrap
- Blister pack coatings
- High-barrier packaging

---

## Glass

### Type I Glass (Borosilicate)

**Properties**:
- Chemical resistance: Excellent
- Thermal shock resistance: Excellent
- Alkali release: Minimal

**Applications**:
- Pharmaceutical injectables
- High-value products
- Laboratory containers

---

### Type II Glass (Treated Soda-Lime)

**Properties**:
- Chemical resistance: Good
- Cost: Lower than Type I
- Surface treatment: Sulfur dioxide

**Applications**:
- Pharmaceuticals (oral)
- Cosmetics
- Food products

---

### Type III Glass (Soda-Lime)

**Properties**:
- Chemical resistance: Moderate
- Cost: Lowest
- Most common glass type

**Applications**:
- Beverages
- Food jars
- General packaging

**Colors**: Clear, amber (UV protection), green, blue

**Recyclability**: ∞ (Infinitely recyclable)

---

## Metal Materials

**Properties**:
- Density: 2.70 g/cm³
- Barrier: Complete (light, oxygen, moisture)
- Formability: Excellent
- Weight: Light

**Applications**:
- Beverage cans
- Aerosol containers
- Pharmaceutical tubes
- Cosmetic containers

**Coatings**:
- Interior: Epoxy, polyester
- Exterior: Printing inks

**Recyclability**: ∞ (Infinitely recyclable)

---

### Steel (Tinplate)

**Properties**:
- Strength: High
- Barrier: Complete
- Formability: Good
- Magnetic: Yes

**Applications**:
- Food cans
- Aerosol cans
- Paint cans

**Coatings**:
- Tin coating (corrosion protection)
- Lacquers (food contact)

**Recyclability**: ∞ (Infinitely recyclable)

## Regulatory Standards

### FDA (United States)

#### 21 CFR Part 177 - Indirect Food Additives (Polymers)
- **177.1520**: Olefin polymers (PE, PP)
- **177.1630**: Polyethylene terephthalate (PET)
- **177.1640**: Polystyrene (PS)
- **177.1975**: Polyvinyl chloride (PVC)

#### 21 CFR Part 178 - Indirect Food Additives (Adjuvants)
- **178.3297**: Colorants
- **178.3910**: Surface lubricants

#### FCN (Food Contact Notification)
- Pre-market notification system
- Alternative to food additive petition

---

### EU (European Union)

#### Regulation (EC) 1935/2004
- **Framework**: General safety requirements for food contact materials
- **Principles**: No transfer of unsafe levels of substances

#### Regulation (EU) 10/2011
- **Scope**: Plastic food contact materials
- **Positive list**: Authorized substances
- **Migration limits**: Overall and specific
- **Declaration of compliance**: Required

---

### Environmental Regulations

#### REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorization of Chemicals)
- **Scope**: EU chemical regulation
- **SVHC**: Substances of Very High Concern
- **Compliance**: Material suppliers must register substances

#### RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances)
- **Scope**: Restriction of hazardous substances in electrical equipment
- **Restricted**: Lead, mercury, cadmium, hexavalent chromium, PBBs, PBDEs

## Dimensional Standards

### Neck Finishes (Closures)

Common neck finish sizes (diameter/height in mm):
- **18/410**: Small bottles (cosmetics)
- **20/410**: Cosmetic bottles
- **24/410**: Standard cosmetic bottles
- **28/410**: Large cosmetic bottles
- **38/400**: Jars and wide-mouth bottles
- **43/400**: Wide-mouth jars
- **53/400**: Large jars
- **70/400**: Very large jars
- **89/400**: Extra-large jars

### Capacity Ranges

**Small**: 5-50 ml (sample, travel sizes)
**Medium**: 50-150 ml (standard cosmetic sizes)
**Large**: 150-500 ml (body care, shampoo)
**Extra Large**: 500 ml+ (bulk, family sizes)

## Barrier Property Testing

### Oxygen Transmission Rate (OTR)
- **Unit**: cc/m²/day or cc/pkg/day
- **Method**: ASTM D3985, ISO 15105
- **Lower is better** for oxygen-sensitive products

### Water Vapor Transmission Rate (WVTR)
- **Unit**: g/m²/day or g/pkg/day
- **Method**: ASTM F1249, ISO 15106
- **Lower is better** for moisture-sensitive products
