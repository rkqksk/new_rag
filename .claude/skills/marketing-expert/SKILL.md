### 6. Marketing Analytics

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**:

**Traffic Metrics**:
- Organic traffic growth: Target 15-20% MoM
- Paid traffic CTR: 2-4%
- Bounce rate: < 50%
- Time on site: > 2 minutes

**Conversion Metrics**:
- Lead conversion rate: 15-25% (B2B)
- Demo-to-trial conversion: 40-50%
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 20-30%
- Email open rate: 20-30%

**Revenue Metrics**:
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $2K-$10K
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): $50K-$500K
- CLV/CAC ratio: > 5:1
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) growth: 10-15%

**ROI Calculation**:
```
ROI = (Revenue - Marketing Cost) / Marketing Cost × 100%

Example:
Revenue: $500,000
Marketing Cost: $100,000
ROI = ($500,000 - $100,000) / $100,000 × 100% = 400%
```

### 8. Email Marketing

**Email Campaign Types**:

**Newsletter** (Monthly):
- Industry news
- Product updates
- Customer success stories
- Upcoming events

**Nurture Sequence** (7-email drip):
1. Welcome + value proposition
2. Problem identification
3. Solution overview
4. Feature deep-dive
5. Case study
6. Pricing & ROI
7. Limited-time offer

**Segmentation**:
- By industry (packaging, PCB, mold)
- By company size (SMB, Mid-market, Enterprise)
- By engagement level (hot, warm, cold)
- By funnel stage (awareness, consideration, decision)

**Best Practices**:
- Personalization (use {first_name}, company)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clear CTA (1 primary per email)
- A/B test subject lines
- Optimal send time (Tue-Thu, 10 AM)

### 9. Trade Shows & Events

**Event Strategy**:

**Pre-Event** (4-6 weeks before):
- Email announcement to list
- Social media promotion
- Landing page for booth visit scheduling
- Promotional materials preparation

**During Event**:
- Booth demos (live product showcase)
- Lead capture (QR code, badge scanner)
- Giveaways (branded items)
- Networking (competitor research)

**Post-Event** (Within 48 hours):
- Thank you email to booth visitors
- Lead qualification
- Demo scheduling
- Social media recap

**ROI Metrics**:
- Leads captured: 100-500 (depending on event size)
- Cost per lead: $50-$200
- Lead-to-opportunity rate: 10-20%
- Event ROI: 200-400%

## Best Practices

### 1. B2B Marketing Principles
- Focus on value, not features
- Educate, don't sell
- Long sales cycles (3-12 months)
- Multiple decision-makers
- Relationship-driven

### 2. Content Quality Over Quantity
- In-depth, valuable content
- Original research & data
- Expert perspectives
- SEO-optimized
- Shareable format

### 3. Data-Driven Decisions
- Track everything
- A/B test campaigns
- Attribution modeling
- Regular reporting
- Continuous optimization

### 4. Customer-Centric Approach
- Solve customer problems
- Personalized messaging
- Responsive support
- Community building
- Feedback loops
